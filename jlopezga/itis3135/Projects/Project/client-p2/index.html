About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2016 by a small group of passionate student developers who met in the College of Computing's hallways, the UNCC Game Development Club has grown from informal Unity tutorial sessions in empty classrooms to a thriving community of over 200 members. Our founders believed that game development could bring together students from all disciplines, creating a unique space where art meets code, and stories meet systems.

Mission

We strive to empower students to transform their gaming passions into tangible skills and creative projects. By fostering an inclusive environment that welcomes all skill levels and backgrounds, we aim to break down the barriers between different disciplines in game development, enabling our members to learn, collaborate, and innovate together in ways that reflect the modern gaming industry's collaborative nature.

Vision

We envision our club as a springboard for the next generation of game developers, where every member can find their unique role in the gaming industry. Whether creating immersive worlds, crafting compelling narratives, designing engaging mechanics, or building robust systems, we believe that by working together, we can push the boundaries of what student game developers can achieve while building lasting professional relationships that extend beyond graduation.

Game Jam Hall Of Fame

Our talented developers have consistently pushed boundaries in our game jams, creating unforgettable experiences. Notable winners include 'Pixel Dreams,' a surreal platformer that transforms players' thoughts into dynamic level designs, and 'Chromatic Chaos,' a puzzle game where colors influence gravity and physics. These innovative projects showcase our members' creativity and technical expertise, from intricate pixel art to sophisticated gameplay mechanics. Each winning game demonstrates not just programming skill, but the unique vision and storytelling abilities that make game development an art form. These success stories inspire new members and remind us that great games can emerge from just 48 hours of passionate development.
